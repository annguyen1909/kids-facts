## What is a leopard?

A leopard is a **spotted wild cat** known for strength, stealth, and climbing skill. It has a golden coat marked with black rosettes and a long tail that helps with balance in trees.

Leopards belong to the cat family [Felidae](/families/felidae), in the same genus as lions and tigers. Their scientific name is *Panthera pardus*.

## Where do leopards live?

Wild leopards live across **Africa** and parts of **Asia**, from savannas and dry bush to rainforests and mountain slopes. They are adaptable and can live surprisingly close to farms and villages when cover and prey are available.

Today many leopards survive inside **protected parks and reserves** where forests and prey animals are still plentiful.

## What do leopards eat?

Leopards are [carnivores](/diets/carnivore). They hunt antelope, deer, wild pigs, monkeys, and other animals they can ambush. A leopard usually hunts **alone**, often at night.

After a large kill, a leopard may drag the prey into a **tree** to keep it safe from lions and hyenas.

## How do leopards live?

Unlike lions, leopards are **mostly solitary**. Each adult keeps its own home range and marks territory with scent. The main exception is a **mother raising cubs**—she feeds, protects, and teaches them to hunt and climb.

Leopards rest in trees, caves, and thick cover during the day and become more active after sunset.

## Why are leopards at risk?

Leopards are listed as **[Vulnerable](/conservation-status/vulnerable)**. Their numbers are falling in many areas because forests are shrinking, prey is harder to find, and leopards are sometimes killed when they come near livestock.

Protected habitat and anti-poaching patrols help the remaining wild populations survive.

## Life cycle and cubs

A baby leopard is called a **cub**. Cubs are born blind and stay hidden in dense cover while the mother hunts nearby. They learn to stalk, climb, and hunt over about two years before living on their own.

Female leopards raise cubs alone, teaching them the skills needed to survive in forests and grasslands.
